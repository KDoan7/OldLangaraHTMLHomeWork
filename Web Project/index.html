What is Killer Instinct?

Killer Instinct (2013) is the third fighting game in the Killer Instinct series and is currently being developed by Iron Galaxy and published by Microsoft Studios. Killer Instinct is an Xbox One and Windows 10 Exclusive. The game is a reboot of the older Killer Instinct titles but however retains some old plot points from the earlier games. The game is broken up into seasons and is currently on its third season. Each season comes with new characters, stages, music and balance changes.


Why play Killer Instinct?

Well the reason why you should play Killer Instinct is because the game is great and is a very unique entry in the fighting game genre due to its many unique system mechanics and the way the game handles its balancing which we will cover in the basics page. The game is very beginner friendly while still remaining complicated at high level and is very rewarding to play. There is a sense of satisfaction that you get when playing a fighting game that you can't get in other genres, the feeling of outplaying your opponent in a one verse one setting or spending hours perfecting combos and pulling them off in the middle of the match, there isn't a feeling like that anywhere else.
